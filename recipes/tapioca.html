Bacalhau(ba-ca-lhau)

Cod is the most popular fish to eat in Portugal! There are a ton of ways to cook it. One of my favorites is Bacalhau-a-Bras

Bacalhau a Bras

About the dish

Bacalha a Bras is cod separated in slices that is mixed with palha-palha chips and egg yok

It's quick to cook and very tasty. The recipe will be a portion for 2 persons, so please share with someone you like :)

Ingredients

  • 500gr of sliced cod
  • 1 Onion
  • 2/3 of an garlic clove
  • 300gr of palha-palha chips
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Olive Oil
  • 6 eggs + 2 yokes
  • A couple of black olives

Steps

  1. Slice one onion.
  2. Saute olive oil, the onion and bay leaf on a pan.
  3. Mince the garlic and add it to the pan.
  4. Add the sliced cod to the pan and ster.
  5. Take the pan away from the heat and add the palha-palha chips. Mix everything together.
  6. Crack the eggs to a bowl and mix them with some water, pepper and salt. Mix them thoroughly.
  7. Remove the bay leaf from the pan and add the eggs to the pan. Mix it all, if to liquid, cook it a bit longer.
  8. Add some black olives and salsa on top.

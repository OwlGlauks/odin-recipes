Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

After baking these cookies, you will be impatiently waiting In front of the oven!! Trust me! A perfect snack for an energy boost, after or before work.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter(softened)
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. In a bowl, cream your butter by mixing it with the sugar until well combined.
  2. Add 1 egg, vanilla extract and salt to the mix.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix your dry ingredients.
  4. Make a hole in the middle of the dry ingredients and combine until the doe is formed.
  5. Add Chocolate Chips, how many your heart desires.
  6. Chill in the fridge for at least 4 hours.
  7. Pre-heat the oven for 200 degrees Celsius.
  8. In a tray lined with parchment paper, scoop the cookie doe onto the tray. Make sure to space them well
  9. Bake for 8 minutes
  10. Let cool before eating

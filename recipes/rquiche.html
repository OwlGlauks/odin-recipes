Rita's Quiche

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 200ml Cream
  • 400g Fresh Brown Mushrooms
  • 1cup cubed bacon (or other protein)
  • 200g Baby Leaf spinach
  • 1package Puffy Pastry
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 180 degrees Celsius.
  2. In a bowl, mix the dry ingredients.
  3. Then, in another bowl whisk the eggs and sugar.
  4. Add carrots and fold in the dry ingredients.
  5. Add melted butter.
  6. Finally, transfer the batter to a cake tin and bake for around 35 min at 180 degrees Celsius.
